Movement Keys

  • Click and drag anywhere on the screen to look around from your current position.
  • Scroll to zoom in and out.
  • Left arrow: Step left
  • Right arrow: Step right

Rules

  1. Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  2. A disc can only be moved if it is the uppermost disc on a stack.
  3. No disc may be placed on top of a smaller disc.
